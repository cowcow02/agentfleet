Design section 1: Color System & Layout. This shows the full palette, a dashboard mockup in both themes, and the semantic status colors. The light theme is warm off-white with darker teal accents for contrast. The dark theme is lifted to #222228 (much lighter than the current #0f1117) with brighter teal accents for readability. Status colors are slightly adjusted per theme for optimal contrast on each background.
